About Us

"iTea Hub is a cozy space created for tea lovers and thinkers. We serve quality teas in a calm, welcoming environment where people can relax, work, or catch up with friends. Whether you’re here to unwind or share ideas, iTea Hub is a place to slow down and enjoy every sip." .


Our Journey

iTea Hub began with a simple idea — to create a space where people could pause, connect, and enjoy a good cup of tea. What started as a love for tea slowly grew into a hub for conversations, creativity, and comfort. Today, iTea Hub continues to brew quality teas and warm moments, bringing people together one cup at a time.


Our Team

At iTea Hub, our team is made up of passionate tea lovers who believe in great service and warm hospitality. From carefully brewing each cup to creating a friendly, welcoming space, we work together to make every visit special.


Join Us

Love tea and good vibes? Join the iTea Hub family. Whether you’re looking to work, relax, or be part of a warm and creative community, our doors are always open. Come sip, connect, and grow with us.
